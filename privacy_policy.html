Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2024

CertTrack ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by CertTrack.

1. Information Collection and Use

CertTrack is designed to help you track your professional certificates and credentials. To provide this service, the app allows you to enter and store the following information:

  • Certificate titles and issuer names.
  • Issue dates and expiration dates.
  • Renewal prices and earned credits.
  • Links to local files (PDFs/Images) stored on your device.
  • Personal notes related to your certifications.

Strictly Local Storage: All certification data you enter into CertTrack is stored locally on your mobile device. We do not transmit this data to our servers, nor do we have access to it.

2. Third-Party Services

To improve the app experience and support development, CertTrack uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information such as your advertising ID to serve personalized ads.
  • Firebase (Google): Used for analytics and crash reporting (Crashlytics) to help us understand app performance and fix technical issues.

These third parties may collect information used to identify you according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy

3. Permissions

CertTrack may request the following permissions on your device:

  • Storage/Media: To allow you to select and link certificate documents (PDFs or images) from your device's storage.
  • Internet: Required for AdMob advertisements and Firebase performance monitoring.

4. Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security features. We recommend using device encryption and passcodes to protect your information.

5. Children’s Privacy

CertTrack does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
