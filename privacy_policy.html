Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

CertTrack ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected in the CertTrack mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

CertTrack is designed to help professional users track certificates and credentials. The app allows you to store the following information on your device:

  • Certificate titles, issuer names, and provider details.
  • Issue dates, expiration dates, and completion status.
  • Renewal costs and earned credit hours (CEUs).
  • Personal notes and activity logs related to your professional progress.

Strictly Local Storage: By default, all data you enter into CertTrack is stored locally on your mobile device. We do not have access to your personal certification records.

2. Third-Party Services & Billing

To provide core features and support development, CertTrack utilizes the following services:

  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage "Premium" in-app subscriptions. Billing is handled securely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). RevenueCat provides us with anonymized transaction data.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements to users on the free tier. AdMob may utilize your advertising ID to serve relevant ads.
  • Firebase: Used for anonymized analytics and crash reporting to improve app stability.

You can review their privacy policies here:

  • Google/AdMob/Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy

3. Backups and Data Security

If you enable Automatic Backups, your data is exported to a folder of your choice on your device or linked cloud service (e.g., iCloud, Google Drive). You are responsible for the security of these backup files.

Since data is stored locally, we recommend using device encryption and secure passcodes to protect your information.

4. Permissions

CertTrack may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you reminders before your certifications expire.
  • Storage Access: To allow you to save and load database backups and link certificate documents.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon being posted within the app or on our official website.
